About Restaurant .


Welcome to our Restaurant

Salt&Pepper Restaurant has been serving delightful experiences through the art of cooking for four decades. A relaxing space combined with flavourful dishes makes it a first choice for every foodie in town. It provides a wide range of items to choose from and lets everyone indulge in an experience of pleasing their taste buds.
We provides a wide range of cuisines and dishes to choose from so that every foodie in town has their best experience here. We have always won the hearts of our customers with appetizing dishes and friendly behaviour. It is the best choice for everyone who wants to enjoy the best quality food at reasonable prices.

Vision

To offer the very best in quality cuisine and create an un-matched experience in family dining, maintaining the highest standard of quality, hygiene, service and customer satisfaction.

Mission

To repeatedly offer value addition in all spheres thereby inducing repeat visits by patrons. To capture, recreate, and replicate the brand experience in as many places as possible.

Safe storage of food

All food in our restaurant premises must be labelled and dated appropriately and stored in temperature-controlled environments.

Cleaning procedures

All kitchen and restaurant equipment must be meticulously cleaned. Including kitchenware, front of house areas, surfaces, floors, and bins.

Personal hygiene

We implemented this by wearing appropriate uniforms ,regularly washing hands and strict procedures around illnesses for all restaurant employees.

Cross-contamination prevention

It's implemented by using chopping boards&knives,storing raw & cooked foods separately & cleaning all surfaces thoroughly.
